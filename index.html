How to Use...

  • When you load, refresh, or click the 20-sided die, you should get 25 randomized Pokemon with randomized Games in each box of the Bingo Card.
  • You can adjust options like Generation and Game by going to Settings
  • Clicking on the Pokemon image will randomize that specific box.
  • Clicking on the Export as PNG button will generate a PNG image of the Bingo Card.
  • Clicking on the Make a Custom Bingo Card button will take you to a page where you can create your own Custom Bingo Card.
  • Remember to check if the Pokemon is obtainable in the game you are playing!

    • TLDR: click 20-sided die to randomize, change Generation and Game options with Settings, remember to check if Pokemon is obtainable!

Settings





  • When enabled, the generator will only pick Pokémon that belong to the chosen generation or the named game. Click "Apply Filter" to enforce the setting for subsequent randomizations.
